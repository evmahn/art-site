A little bit about us.

We are two artists who work well independently, and create amazing works of art together. Typically we work with acrylic on canvas in many different styles. However, we also use oils, spraypaint, pencils, and house paint. We also enjoy painting on odd surfaces from rocks, to wood, murals, slate, glass, and anything else we (or you) can think of.

We are local to Cleveland Ohio and love to share our creativity with others! If you need help with any artistic conundrums you may have do not be shy and contact us! Things we can help with include murals and indoor painting, filling your home or office with paintings made to match your aesthetic, creative consultation, and anything else you can think of!
